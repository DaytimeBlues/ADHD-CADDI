## Usage Notes

Use these images as:

1. **Reference for designers** - Show the vision
2. **Background assets** - If quality is high enough
3. **Mood boards** - Present to stakeholders
4. **Style guides** - Extract colors and mood

## Recommended AI Tools

- **Midjourney:** Best for artistic interpretation
- **DALL-E 3:** Good for following specific instructions
- **Stable Diffusion:** Best for fine control and iteration
- **Adobe Firefly:** Good for commercial use

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Iterate:** Generate 10-20 variations, pick the best
2. **Combine:** Use elements from multiple generations
3. **Upscale:** Use AI upscalers for higher resolution
4. **Edit:** Use Photoshop/Figma to refine
5. **Animate:** Use After Effects or Rive for motion

## Color Extraction

From generated images, extract:

- Sky colors (3-4 gradient stops)
- Uluru rock colors (day/sunset/night variants)
- Star colors (white, blue-white, gold)
- Sand colors (ochre, sienna, rust)
- Accent colors (fire glow, moonlight)

Use tools like:

- Adobe Color
- Coolors.co
- Color Thief (from images)
